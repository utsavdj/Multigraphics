Who We Are

-Serving since 1995

As a pioneer printing press in Nepal we started with few employees, hand printing and some machine printing, and now we have grown out to become one of the biggest and most successful printing company.

Our diligence to maintain the highest standards in our prints, commitment to research and technology, and willingness to listen to our customers have helped us to successfully sustain and maintain strong presence in the competitive printing industry through strong annual growth and the highest levels of customer satisfaction.

We have experience working with some of the biggest names in printing press, Heidelberg and Komori, and currently use Komori for our prints. We also have experience working with numerous clients from various fields and satisfying their printing need on time.

We are here to become your long-term printing partner and are capable of providing our services to you around the world.

We Offer

Top Shelf Prints

In Various Forms

To maintain control over the quality of our prints we perform most of our services in-house. In order to do so we have our own four color and two color printing machines, digital color scanners and printers, cutting machines, employees form the community to bind papers, graphic designers and we develop our own printing plates.

We also help you customize your prints by offering choices of various papers of different sizes, thickness and textures, shades of colors, dye cuts, lamination and binds.

Our Team

Get to know the people who you will be working with

Mohan Dhoj Joshi

Chief Executive Officer

Mohan has been in the printing business for a very long time. He went to Holland to study the printing business in the late 80's and returned to Nepal after studies. He has been with Multi Graphics Press since the very beginning and currently owns the business. Mohan is also an entrepreneur who is involved in the automotive and real-estate business. Besides earning from his businesses he also provides his earnings for the society as a Rotarian.

We would not have been where we currently are without our precious team of employees who work hard to satisfy our customer needs. Most of our employees are from our local community and we are proud to have them with us.
